Raw Data

This page displays the raw data from 11/29/2017 - 11/29/2018 used to make the graphs.

The stock data was sourced using google finance. The option data was sourced from a curated list of option trades output by Trade-alert.com. The option orders are filtered based on a proprietary algorithm designed to find aggressive option order flow. The option orders are assumed to be long calls. Regardless of the assumptions made by the algorithms responsible for the call option data, there is no guarantee any party besides the owner of the options understands the intentions behind the trade. Through careful analysis, I hope to uncover patterns in the data to make informed trading decisions.
